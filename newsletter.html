Grand Knight's Message


Brother Knights,

As I sit here in front of the Blessed Sacrament, I can’t help to think about how thankful I am that the Lord has blessed me so much in my life. I am a sinner unworthy of His love but yet He keeps welcoming me back with open arms, time after time. I am so thankful God has chosen me during Baptism and provided me two parents who taught me the Way of the Lord and how to love. I am thankful to have been blessed with my soulmate and three loving children that helped me define my life. Thankful to have a job that allows me to provide for my family. Thankful to have met Father Sy when my faith was stuck in neutral. Thankful to have followed up on an invitation to join the Knights after an unusual dinner recruitment. Thankful for the self-confidence and self-discipline 40 for 40 taught me. Thankful for Past Grand Knights who help guide me, answer all my questions and even help me with cooking. Thankful for the United States and our Veterans who have and continue to fight for our freedom. Last but not least, thankful for my Brothers who always answer the call for help, accept me for who I am and make me a better father, a better husband, and a better man.

A Blessed and Happy Thanksgiving wish for you and your families.

Vivat Jesus,
George Alfaro
Grand Knight
St. Martin de Porres Council 8238
